Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2024

Introduction

NestBud Gardening ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting personally identifiable information you may provide us through the Website. We have adopted this privacy policy to explain what information may be collected on our Website, how we use this information, and under what circumstances we may disclose the information to third parties.

Detailed Privacy Policy Points

1. Information Collection

We collect various types of information when you interact with our services, including personal identification information (name, email, phone number), demographic information, payment details, and technical data about your device and browsing behavior. This collection occurs through direct interactions, automated technologies, and third-party sources to provide and improve our services while maintaining security standards.

2. Use of Collected Data

Your information enables us to process transactions, provide customer support, personalize your experience, and improve our services. We analyze usage patterns to enhance website functionality, develop new features, and conduct market research. Data may also be used for security purposes, fraud prevention, and compliance with legal obligations as required by applicable laws.

3. Data Sharing Practices

We may share information with trusted service providers who assist in business operations under strict confidentiality agreements. Data might be disclosed during business transfers, to comply with legal processes, or to protect rights and safety. We never sell personal information to third-party marketers and implement measures to ensure all sharing complies with this policy.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to enhance functionality, analyze trends, and personalize content. These tools help remember preferences, understand user behavior, and deliver targeted advertisements. You can control cookie settings through your browser, though disabling certain cookies may affect website performance and features.

5. Data Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security protocols including encryption, firewalls, and access controls to protect your information. Regular security audits and employee training help maintain data integrity. While we strive to protect your personal information, no electronic transmission or storage method is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. User Rights and Choices

You have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information in our systems. Requests can be made through our contact form, and we will respond within 30 days. You may opt-out of marketing communications, restrict certain data processing, or withdraw consent where applicable, subject to legal or contractual restrictions.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent. If we become aware of such collection, we will take steps to delete the information promptly. Parents or guardians can contact us to review, delete, or restrict processing of a child's information.

8. International Data Transfers

As a global business, we may transfer and process data outside your country of residence. When transferring data across borders, we implement appropriate safeguards as required by applicable laws, including standard contractual clauses or other approved mechanisms. By using our services, you consent to such transfers.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites not operated by us. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites they visit. This policy applies solely to information collected by our services.

10. Data Retention Periods

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. Criteria used to determine retention periods include legal requirements, dispute resolution needs, and business operations. Anonymized data may be retained indefinitely for analytical purposes.

11. California Privacy Rights

California residents have specific rights under the CCPA, including rights to know about personal information collected, disclosed, or sold, and the right to request deletion of personal information. We do not sell personal information as defined by CCPA. California consumers can make requests through designated channels outlined in our CCPA supplement.

12. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area, we comply with GDPR requirements regarding lawful processing, data subject rights, and international transfers. Our legal bases for processing include consent, contract performance, legal obligations, and legitimate interests. EU residents have additional rights including data portability and objection to processing.

13. Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, bankruptcy, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction. We will notify you via email and/or prominent website notice of any change in ownership or uses of your personal information, as well as any choices you may have regarding your information.

14. Automated Decision Making

We may use automated processing and profiling to provide personalized services, prevent fraud, and analyze user behavior. When such processing has legal or significant effects, we implement appropriate safeguards, provide human review options, and obtain explicit consent where required by applicable laws.

15. Do Not Track Signals

Our website currently does not respond to Do Not Track browser settings or signals. However, we offer opt-out mechanisms for specific tracking technologies through our cookie preferences center. You can manage tracking through your browser settings, though this may impact functionality of certain website features.

16. Social Media Integration

Our services may include social media features that collect your IP address and page visitation data. These features are hosted by third parties, and interactions are governed by their privacy policies. We may receive aggregated demographic information from social platforms to enhance our marketing strategies and service offerings.

17. Email Communications

We send transactional emails and, with consent, marketing communications. All emails include unsubscribe options, and we honor opt-out requests promptly. We track email open rates and click-through behavior to improve content relevance. Your email address is never shared with third parties except service providers facilitating these communications.

18. Mobile Applications

Our mobile apps may collect device identifiers, location data, and usage statistics to provide location-based services and troubleshoot technical issues. You can control app permissions through your device settings. Certain features may require specific permissions to function properly, which will be clearly explained during installation.

19. Payment Processing

Payment transactions are processed by PCI-DSS compliant third-party processors. We do not store complete credit card information after transaction completion. Tokenized payment methods may be retained for recurring billing with your authorization. Financial data is encrypted during transmission using SSL technology and other industry-standard protocols.

20. User-Generated Content

Content you post publicly on our platforms may be viewed and collected by others. We are not responsible for personal information you choose to include in such content. We may use, modify, and display user-generated content for promotional purposes across all media platforms, with attribution unless otherwise requested.

21. Surveys and Research

Participation in surveys or research studies is voluntary, and responses may be used for improving services or published in aggregated form. Personal identifiers are removed from research data when possible. Survey responses may be linked to your account to enhance personalization unless you opt for anonymous participation.
